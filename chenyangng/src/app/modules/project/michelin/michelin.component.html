Overview

The context of the "connected vehicles" is a new business model proposed and implemented by Atos Worldline. In 2013, Worldline assist MICHELIN速 solutions, a Michelin group specializing in fleet mobility, with the implementation of innovative connected solutions for B2B customers. The Worldline solution Connected Vehicles gives MICHELIN速 solutions the ability to propose new value-added solutions to fleets.

In the "Fleet administration platform" (A supply chain management platform which preview, oder, monitor and supervise the installations and stocks), the support team lack a tool that can automatically unify data from different platforms and provides integrated information on a unique interface. For example, the static information of vehicles in the platform and alerting of functional inconsistency from different platforms(Machine To Machine, Fleet Management, Context Broker..).

My mission was to analyze the existing problems and implement innovative components that assist support team to diagnose, monitor and visualize the statistic information of the platform

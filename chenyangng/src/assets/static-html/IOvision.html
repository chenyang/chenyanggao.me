RIA IOvision: R&D of prototype integration for small business ERP "IOvision"

My final year internship (and now I work with working contract) took place in a French IT service company located in Montpellier. My mission was to research, modeling and develop a Rich Client prototype for company's current business and project management software "IOvision". Instructed by lead system engineer and worked with other colleagues, I successfully implemented the prototpe by using AngularJS Framework with different front-end technologies and JEE Web Services validated by unit testing. Besides, I regularly participated in prototype's validation checkpoints and meetings. Note:I am not in a position to present any detailed information, including but not limiting to application core features, business strategic plans and product details.

Overview

IOcean is a locally based IT service company created since 2001, whose main focus is software engineering, opensource CMS projets (JEE, Symphony, Dupral, Typo3, Liferay, Titanium..) and web projets constructions. Besides, company operates and manage a small business ERP IOvision which exists since 2003 and owns 1000+ active users.

My mission was to research and analysis a sub-module "Commercial-contact" and "Welcome module"(including menu, dashboard, navigation control..) to

1.Follow instructions to change and improve current system's ergonomy and graphisme for a better user experience (respecting responsive design)

2.Modeling and implementation of the new design with MVVM Framework AngularJS and different client side technologies

3.Adding and integrating a new layer JEE Web Service (JAX-RS, JAX-WS) based on system's current framework 'ILoa'(JSP MVC2)

4. Integration with front-end realization with new web services and finalize the prototype.


Module analysis & technology choosing

During the phase of module analysis(A sub module of "Commercial-contact"), I worked with "IOvision"'s' project manager and the senior system engineer to discover and research its fonctionnalities. Business suite "IOvision" is a small business ERP for commerce, project, HR and internal affaires management, which includes 6 main modules, hundreds of tables and fonctionalities.

In order to estalish a scalable web application, we have chosen AngularJS as its front-end Framework, which is extendible, flexible, easy-to-use and well architectured especially dedicated to develop large size web application, for example its MVVM design pattern, idea of dependency injection, directives, ease of testing etc.. This architecture change makes this small business ERP move towards to philosophy "Front-end focused"(such as ergonomy, graphique, business logic, web services) and ease the "tight coupling" from traditional MVC model.

Architecture design and front-end implementation with framework AngularJS

After we have decided to use AngularJS as main front-end framework, instructed by lead system engineer, I developed and established the architecture of the prototype, included but not limited to AngularJS modules(Controller, services, directives and filters), HTML modules, CSS modules and third-party integration such as grunt server and bower. For unit testing, I used Jasmine to cover the code and I reached 95% coverage of AngularJS modules.

Technically, I used Grunt Server and bower to manage third-party Javascripts components and front-end plugins, and JS utilities such as underscroeJS and d3js/nvd3, jQplot(abandonned) to generate dashboard components. Concerning about AngularJS, I used third-party vendors such as AngularUI, AngularBoostrap, AngularStrap to generate components like grid/table components. Besides, during the project I broadly used "Dependency injection", "Routage", "Factory & Services", "Events & Exception", "Promise" in Angularjs and self-defined directives.

Generally, I participated regularly the validation checkpoints and meetings with project manager and system engineers to discuss and follow the work progress. The project is putted on Git and I got many significant experience of team-working after my supervisor's checking codes and quality assurance. Besides, I follow and manage my tasks on company's internal task management platform "Redmine".

Developement of JEE web service and Integration with current ERP's Framework "ILoa"

The original ERP's architecture was invented and implemented by 2 senior system engineers in 2003 which is called "ILoa", a similar JSP MVC Model2 but with improved datastore and tag technologies.

Brief Intro of system architecture

As ERP "IOvision" has been used and established for about 10 years, changing all its backend infrastructure (like change to Spring and Hibernate integration) is impossible and not allowed by the budget. What my principle mission was to add and integrate a new JEE Web service layer to communicate with front-end reliazation (RESTFul services). Instructed by my supervisor and worked with founder of framework ILoa, I successfully added a layer of JEE Web service (Tomcat+SVN+Maven, by using JAX-RS, JAXB, Jackson..) with AngularJS integration. For persistence issues, it's easy to call ILOA's persistence module funtions and it will automatically integrate with Sybase database.

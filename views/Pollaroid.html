Pollaroid: iOS Social Network development

During my internship in Singapore summer 2012, I worked as an iOS developer for a strategic applicaiton development. Since the applicaiton is now under development (company is still working on this) and not yet published to Apple Store, I am not in a position to present any detailed information, including but not limiting to application core features, business strategic plans and product details.

Overview

Sageby Pte Ltd is a technology start-up funded by SPRING Singapore which provides the platform to reach people over the physical and social space almost anywhere and anytime, in the form of data collection. Sageby provide the linkage to make cab rides, shopping, and dining free for our users by helping them monetize idle time. Company project extends to Business Intelligence, Survey engine and Mobile platform.

Worked with designer and Database developer, we built a working prototype for alpha testing of iOS application "Pollaroid" before my leaving of the company. During my internship, I held full responsibility for app Object-C (Cocoa Touch) development. My works involved deeply in brain storming, story board and implementation phases. Also I designed application architecture and Database Model.

System Architecture

In general, the application is "module oriented", which means we divided the system by its funciton modules. In the eye of UI, we seperate the application by tabs with navigation embedded. System architecture is simple, we used JSON as common data format to communicate with remote php files who are responsible for stored procedures and interaction with database on the server.

Cocoa Touch Development

During this phase about 2 month, mainly I worked with database developer and designer (Everyone were so busy), at the end of this phase we worked out a prototype for alpha testing. During this phase, I held the whole responsibility for Cocoa Touch Framework development, included but not limited to system architecture modeling, web support of API, Cocoa Touch UI, Navigation and Tab, Core Location, Camera API and Social Network integration with Facebook and Foursquare.

Product Launching

For product launching, we tested on iPhone 4s and received many user feedback. Some were supportive and some were negative. During the development phase, we changed the design and the front-end from time to time.

Usibility testing and user feedback

Since the applicaiton was not totally launched and developped, we just made a alpha Beta testing for application. We invited 3 people to test our application and they gave us feedbacks. We tested over 1. whether application met the requirements that guided design and development;2. wether application worked as expected;3. wether application can be implemented with the same characteristics. The result turned that we had many bugs and we changed design also.

Allons-y Francais: French Teaching Videos

During my internship in Allons-y Francais, besides the work done on CSS and Javascript, with the assistance of my instructor, I recorded and fixed French teaching videos of the school using Adobe Premiere combined with ppt files.

Overview

During summber 2010, I worked as an IT assistance intern in the department of "Formation a distance" in Allons-y Centre de Formation Francais, a leading french teaching school in Beijing. I helped my supervisor, Mr CUI managed and fixed hundreds of teaching materials, worked on website HTML and Javascript programming works, fixed images taken in class and meetings and also I participated in many company public affairs such as "CAFA-Conference Vin du Medoc Bordeaux" and "Beijing international education exhibition".

Besides my small works conducted, I worked mainly on the project "French Teaching Viedo Allons-y Francais".This was a commercial project which was initiated by the president of the School Mr LI. Under my supervisor's instruction, I learned how to manipulate the video projector and I learned basically the usage of Adobe Premiere.

Realiztion & related works

For the video recording and fixing, I have recorded and fixed
-"Reflet" from Lession 1 to Lession 15
-"Cours de francais accelerer" from Lession 13 to Lesson 30
-"Francais pour les enfants" from Lession 1 to Lesson 9
- Besides, I fixed and conducted all ppt files for Lessions.
